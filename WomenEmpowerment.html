WHY Women EMPOWERMENT

Institute of Social Awareness & Reforms ISAR-Yavatmal is a registered, non-profit, secular, voluntary organization working in the field of rural development since 1994. ISAR specific objectives to solve the livelihood, health, education, social and watershed issues, which are greatly interlinked. Its registered office is at Kumbha a village in Maregaon block of Yavatmal district in Maharashtra. Organisation is working with most ignored, socially and economically oppressed communities to achieve the sustainable development with a focus on women empowerment. Empowering community based organizations and work with them for sustainable development especially by managing local resources are major concerns of the organization.

Organization of women through self-help groups

ISAR facilitated for forming and federation women self-help groups and capacitated through various interventions. Apart form that it is ensured that the women become integral part of the decision-making process at village level and also resolve issues the women's facing internal problems. They are also involved in the development process going on at village level.

Participation in governance

Women federation has been established in the working area of ISAR 318 women are the members of this federation. ISAR has always focused in the issue of women participation in the decision-making processes at village level. Organization has made the conscious efforts in organizing women in the form of women Gramsabhas at eh village level. In villages women Gram sabhas are being conducted regularly and also these women participate in general gram Sabha’s and share their issues and contribute actively in development process. Management plans and conservation of the villages are reviewed by women regularly, Women have also become the integral part of Gram Sabha Mahasangh organized at block level.
